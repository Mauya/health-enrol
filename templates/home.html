Welcome to The Faculty of Health

This dashboard shows all student enrolled in the past 5 years in the Faculty of Health.


The interactive dashboards allows live enrolment data to be assessed against defined internal targets and compared to enrolment data from previous years.


The dashboard shows enrolment and withdrawal numbers.


The select menu allows multiple filtering options for analysing data by key indicators such as gender, ethnicity and or international/overseas students.


The dashboard highlights trends and help predict future expectations in terms of student numbers
